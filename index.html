The Voice Behind

Disha Sehdev

Author & Storyteller

With a pen as my compass and imagination as my map, I traverse the landscapes of human emotion, weaving tales that resonate with the soul. Each story is a journey, each word a stepping stone to worlds both familiar and fantastical.

My writing explores the delicate threads that connect us all—love, loss, hope, and the eternal search for meaning. Through fiction, poetry, and reflective essays, I invite readers to pause, ponder, and perhaps see the world through a different lens.
